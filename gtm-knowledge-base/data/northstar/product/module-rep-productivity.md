# Rep Productivity

Rep Productivity connects what reps *do* to what reps *close*. It moves the conversation past raw activity counts to activity that actually drives pipeline and revenue — and gives managers the evidence to coach.

## Core capabilities

### Activity-to-outcome analytics
Northstar correlates activities (calls, emails, meetings, multi-threading) with outcomes (pipeline created, stage progression, win rate) so you can see which behaviors move deals for *your* team — not generic best practices. Managers stop rewarding dials for the sake of dials.

### Ramp tracking
Track new reps against a defined ramp curve: time to first meeting, first pipeline, first close, and full quota. Northstar flags reps ahead of or behind the curve early enough to intervene. Customers commonly cut ramp time by around three weeks.

### Quota attainment analytics
See attainment distribution across the team, not just the average. Identify the "healthy middle" vs a top-heavy team carried by two reps, and see how attainment tracks with pipeline coverage and activity mix.

### Coaching insights
Each rep gets a scorecard: pipeline hygiene, forecast accuracy (from the [Forecast Accuracy](module-forecast-accuracy.md) module), activity mix, and conversion by stage. Managers walk into 1:1s with a data-backed agenda instead of anecdotes.

## Outcomes

Teams that adopt Rep Productivity typically see about a **12% lift in quota attainment** within a year and materially faster ramp for new hires. See the [Series D vertical SaaS case study](../case-studies/series-d-vertical-saas.md).

## How it works

Rep Productivity draws activity data from the CRM and, where connected, from [Gong, Outreach, and Salesloft](integrations.md). It models everything in your Snowflake or BigQuery warehouse alongside pipeline and forecast data, so activity and outcomes live in one place. Syncs are hourly.
