# Pricing

Northstar is priced for the mid-market — transparent tiers, billed annually, with a proof-of-value pilot so you buy on evidence, not a demo.

## Tiers

### Core — $2,500 / month
- Up to **40** revenue-team seats
- **Pipeline Analytics** + **Forecast Accuracy** modules
- Standard integrations: Salesforce **or** HubSpot, Snowflake **or** BigQuery
- Slack alerts, hourly syncs
- Email support

### Growth — $6,000 / month
- Up to **120** seats
- Everything in Core **plus the Rep Productivity module**
- **All integrations** (Gong, Outreach, Salesloft, Looker, Tableau, Mode, Marketo)
- Custom dashboards, quarterly business reviews
- Priority support

### Enterprise — custom
- **Unlimited** seats
- **Warehouse-native deployment** — your data never leaves your Snowflake/BigQuery
- SSO/SAML + SCIM, advanced RBAC, audit-log export
- 15-minute near-real-time syncs
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager, custom data models, SLA
- SOC 2 Type II report access

## Add-ons & terms

- **Additional seats** beyond the tier limit: **$25 / seat / month**.
- **Rep Productivity** can be added to Core as an à-la-carte module.
- Annual billing; multi-year discounts available on Enterprise.

## Proof-of-value pilot

Every new customer starts with a **14-day proof-of-value pilot** on a slice of live pipeline. We agree on a success metric up front — usually forecast accuracy or review-prep time — and you decide based on your own data. Full deployment then takes **4–6 weeks**.

## How to think about ROI

Most customers justify Northstar on two numbers from our [case studies](../case-studies/series-c-fintech.md): reaching **90%+ forecast accuracy within two quarters** and cutting weekly **pipeline-review prep from ~6 hours to ~30 minutes** across the management team. For a team of 40, review-prep savings alone typically exceed the Core subscription.

Compared with enterprise incumbents like [Clari](../sales/battlecard-clari.md), Northstar lands well under six figures at the Core and Growth tiers and deploys in weeks, not quarters. See [positioning](../sales/positioning.md) and the [FAQ](../sales/faq.md) for common pricing questions.
