# Northstar Analytics — Product Overview

**Northstar Analytics is the RevOps analytics layer for teams outgrowing spreadsheets and BI tools not built for revenue data.** We sit on top of your CRM and warehouse and give revenue teams a single, trustworthy source of truth for pipeline, forecasting, and rep productivity.

## The problem we solve

Between $20M and $200M ARR, revenue reporting breaks. The CRM holds the deals, the warehouse holds the source data, and a general-purpose BI tool (Looker, Tableau, Mode) or a wall of spreadsheets holds everything in between. Forecasts get rebuilt by hand every week, pipeline hygiene is nobody's job, and leadership stops trusting the numbers. Companies at this stage have usually just hired their first RevOps leader — and handed them a mess.

Northstar is built for exactly that moment.

## What Northstar does

Northstar connects to Salesforce or HubSpot and to Snowflake or BigQuery, models your revenue data once, and delivers three tightly integrated modules:

- **[Pipeline Analytics](module-pipeline-analytics.md)** — pipeline hygiene scoring, stage-conversion analysis, deal inspection, and slippage/movement tracking so nothing rots silently.
- **[Forecast Accuracy](module-forecast-accuracy.md)** — a submission-and-roll-up workflow with AI-assisted predictions, commit-vs-best-case scenarios, and forecast-vs-actual scoring over time.
- **[Rep Productivity](module-rep-productivity.md)** — activity-to-outcome analytics, ramp tracking, quota attainment, and coaching insights.

## Why teams pick us

- **Warehouse-native.** Northstar runs on the data you already trust in Snowflake or BigQuery. On Enterprise, your data never leaves your warehouse.
- **Fast to value.** Typical deployment is **4–6 weeks**, not two quarters.
- **Transparent.** Every forecast number traces back to the deals and rules behind it — no black box.
- **Built for RevOps, priced for the mid-market.** See [pricing](pricing.md).

## Proof

Customers typically reach **90%+ forecast accuracy within two quarters** and cut weekly **pipeline-review prep from ~6 hours to ~30 minutes**. See our [case studies](../case-studies/series-c-fintech.md).

## Who it's for

B2B SaaS companies, 200–2000 employees, Series B to Series D, running Salesforce/HubSpot plus Snowflake/BigQuery. Primary buyers are the **VP of RevOps, Head of Sales Operations, CRO, and VP of Sales**. Full detail in the [ICP definition](../sales/icp-definition.md).
