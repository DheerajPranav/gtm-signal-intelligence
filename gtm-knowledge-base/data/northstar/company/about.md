# About Northstar Analytics

> *Northstar Analytics is a fictional company created for GTM AI training and demos. See the [corpus README](../README.md).*

## What we do

Northstar Analytics is **the RevOps analytics layer for teams outgrowing spreadsheets and BI tools not built for revenue data.** We sit on top of a company's CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot) and data warehouse (Snowflake or BigQuery) and give revenue teams one trustworthy source of truth for **pipeline hygiene, forecasting, and rep productivity** — through our [three modules](../product/overview.md).

## The company

- **Founded:** 2019.
- **Headquarters:** San Francisco, CA — remote-first across the US and EU.
- **Funding:** Series B; **$32M raised** to date.
- **Team:** ~120 employees.
- **Customers:** 140+ B2B SaaS companies.

## Who we serve

Our [ideal customer](../sales/icp-definition.md) is a **B2B SaaS company, 200–2000 employees, Series B–D**, that has just hired a RevOps leader and is outgrowing spreadsheets for revenue reporting. We're opinionated for that stage — not a heavyweight enterprise or finance platform.

## Why we exist

Between $20M and $200M ARR, revenue reporting breaks. The CRM has the deals, the warehouse has the data, and a spreadsheet holds the forecast together with tape. We started Northstar to give the revenue team the same kind of trustworthy, warehouse-native analytics layer that data teams already expect — so the forecast becomes a measured process, not a guess.

## How we're different

Warehouse-native, transparent, and priced for the mid-market. Every forecast number traces to the deals behind it, and on Enterprise your data never leaves your warehouse. See our [positioning](../sales/positioning.md).
