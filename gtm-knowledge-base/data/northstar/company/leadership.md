- **Maya Chen — Co-founder & CEO.** Set the original thesis that the revenue team deserves a warehouse-native analytics layer of its own. Leads company strategy and fundraising.
- **Diego Ramos — Co-founder & CTO.** Owns the product architecture, including the warehouse-native deployment model and the transparent forecast engine.

- **Marcus Webb — Chief Revenue Officer (CRO).** Runs sales and the go-to-market motion; owns the [sales playbook](../sales/sales-playbook.md) and forecast discipline internally.
- **Elena Kowalski — VP of Marketing.** Leads positioning, demand, and content; owns the [messaging](../sales/positioning.md) and [marketing site](../marketing/homepage.md).
- **Sam Okafor — VP of Customer Success.** Owns onboarding, the 4–6 week implementation, and the proof-of-value pilot experience.

## How the team operates

Northstar is **remote-first** across the US and EU, founded in 2019 and headquartered in San Francisco. The leadership team runs the company on its own product — the internal forecast goes through the same [submission-and-scoring workflow](../product/module-forecast-accuracy.md) customers use.

## Culture notes (for GTM context)

- **Honest by default.** We don't oversell — the [pricing](../product/pricing.md), [security posture](../product/security.md), and integration limits are stated plainly, including what we *don't* do.
- **Warehouse-native is a belief, not a feature.** The conviction that data should stay where the customer already trusts it shapes both product and sales.
