# Customers & Reference Accounts

> *All customers below are fictional, created for GTM AI training/demos.*

Northstar serves **140+ B2B SaaS companies**, primarily Series B–D, 200–2000 employees, on Salesforce/HubSpot + Snowflake/BigQuery. Below are the named reference accounts with published [case studies](../case-studies/series-c-fintech.md).

## Reference accounts

| Customer | Segment | Stack | Modules | Headline result |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Ledgerly** | Series C fintech (~450 emp) | Salesforce + Snowflake | Pipeline, Forecast | Forecast accuracy **68% → 91%** in two quarters — [case study](../case-studies/series-c-fintech.md) |
| **Forgestack** | Series B devtools (~230 emp) | HubSpot + BigQuery | Pipeline, Forecast | Stood up RevOps from scratch in **5 weeks** — [case study](../case-studies/series-b-devtools.md) |
| **Cliniva** | Series D healthcare SaaS (~1,400 emp) | Salesforce + Snowflake | All three (Enterprise) | **+12% quota attainment**, ramp **−3 weeks** — [case study](../case-studies/series-d-vertical-saas.md) |
| **Adloom** | Series C marketing tech (~600 emp) | Salesforce + BigQuery | Pipeline, Forecast | Forecast accuracy **+19 points** — [case study](../case-studies/series-c-marketing-tech.md) |

## Segments we're strong in

- **Fintech & payments** (e.g., Ledgerly) — high scrutiny on forecast accuracy and board reporting.
- **Developer tools & infrastructure** (e.g., Forgestack) — teams building RevOps for the first time.
- **Vertical SaaS** (e.g., Cliniva) — larger orgs needing warehouse-native governance and rep productivity.
- **Marketing & ad tech** (e.g., Adloom) — volatile pipelines needing one reconciled number.

## Using references

Match the prospect to the closest reference by **stage, stack, and pain**. A first-time RevOps hire maps to Forgestack; a forecast-credibility problem maps to Ledgerly or Adloom; a rep-productivity/scale problem maps to Cliniva.
