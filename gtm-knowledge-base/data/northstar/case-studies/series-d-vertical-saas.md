# Case Study: Cliniva

> *Fictional customer for training/demo purposes.*

**Company:** Cliniva — a Series D vertical SaaS platform for healthcare practices, ~1,400 employees, Salesforce + Snowflake.
**Modules:** Pipeline Analytics, Forecast Accuracy, Rep Productivity (full Growth → Enterprise).
**Headline result:** **+12% quota attainment** across the team within a year and **new-rep ramp shortened by ~3 weeks**.

## The problem

Cliniva had a large, growing sales org where quota attainment was carried by a handful of top reps. Leadership couldn't see **which activities actually created pipeline**, and new reps took too long to become productive. They had outgrown a legacy reporting setup and wanted rep-level insight without buying a heavyweight enterprise platform.

## Why Northstar

At Series D scale with strict data requirements, Cliniva needed **warehouse-native** deployment so data never left their Snowflake environment ([security](../product/security.md)) plus [Rep Productivity](../product/module-rep-productivity.md) analytics to diagnose the attainment gap. Northstar's Enterprise tier fit the [pricing](../product/pricing.md) and governance needs (SSO/SAML, SCIM, dedicated CSM).

## What changed

- Deployed **warehouse-native** on Snowflake; data never left Cliniva's environment.
- Used [Rep Productivity](../product/module-rep-productivity.md) to identify the activity patterns of top performers and coach the rest of the team against them.
- Built a ramp dashboard so managers could see new-rep progress against a productivity baseline.

## Results

- **+12% quota attainment** across the team within a year.
- **New-rep ramp shortened by ~3 weeks.**
- Attainment less dependent on a few heroes; coaching grounded in what top reps actually do.

> "We finally know which rep behaviors create pipeline — and we can coach to them." — VP of Sales, Cliniva *(illustrative)*
