# Battlecard: Northstar vs Gong Forecast

## Who they are

Gong is a **conversation-intelligence** leader; **Gong Forecast** is the forecasting product that sits on top of that platform. Its center of gravity is call recording and deal intelligence from conversations.

## Where they win

- Excellent conversation intelligence; if a team is already all-in on Gong, Forecast is a natural add-on.
- Rich call/email signal feeding deal warnings.
- Strong brand with sales reps and managers.

## Where we win (lead with these)

- **Purpose-built for revenue data, not a bolt-on.** Forecasting is secondary to Gong's core CI product; for Northstar it's the core. Our submission workflow, AI forecast, and forecast-vs-actual scoring are deeper.
- **You don't have to buy the whole platform.** To get Gong Forecast you're effectively adopting conversation intelligence and its per-seat cost. Northstar delivers a real forecast without requiring a CI rollout.
- **Warehouse-native.** Northstar models in Snowflake/BigQuery and reconciles with your data team's numbers; Gong's data lives in Gong.
- **Transparent forecast.** Every Northstar number traces to deals and weights; we score accuracy over time. See [Forecast Accuracy](../product/module-forecast-accuracy.md).
- **We integrate with Gong** rather than compete with its strength — import [Gong activity](../product/integrations.md) to enrich deal inspection while owning the forecast layer.

## Traps to set

- "Is forecasting the core of the product, or a feature on top of call recording?"
- "Do you have to roll out conversation intelligence to every rep to get the forecast?"
- "Can your forecast reconcile with what your data team sees in the warehouse?"

## Objections you'll hear

- *"Gong already has our call data."* — Great — keep it, and let Northstar import that activity while owning a purpose-built, warehouse-native forecast.
- *"One vendor is simpler."* — Only if forecasting is good enough as a side feature. For a team that lives or dies on forecast accuracy, depth wins.

## One-liner

**Gong Forecast is a forecasting feature on a conversation-intelligence platform; Northstar is a purpose-built, warehouse-native forecast that plays nicely with Gong.**
