# Positioning & Messaging

## One-liner

**Northstar Analytics is the RevOps analytics layer for teams outgrowing spreadsheets and BI tools not built for revenue data.**

## Category

Northstar is **RevOps analytics / revenue intelligence for the data layer**. We are not a CRM, not a conversation-intelligence tool, and not a finance-planning platform. We are the analytics layer that turns CRM + warehouse data into trustworthy pipeline, forecast, and productivity insight for the revenue team.

## The three-sentence pitch

Between $20M and $200M ARR, revenue reporting breaks: the CRM has the deals, the warehouse has the data, and spreadsheets hold the forecast together with tape. Northstar sits on top of Salesforce/HubSpot and Snowflake/BigQuery and gives revenue teams one trustworthy source of truth for pipeline hygiene, forecasting, and rep productivity. Customers reach 90%+ forecast accuracy within two quarters and cut weekly review prep from six hours to thirty minutes.

## Value pillars

1. **Trustworthy forecast.** Transparent, AI-assisted, scored against actuals over time. Not a black box.
2. **Warehouse-native.** Runs on the data you already trust; on Enterprise your data never leaves your warehouse. Fast security review, fast deploy (4–6 weeks).
3. **Built for RevOps, priced for the mid-market.** Opinionated for this stage, not a heavyweight enterprise or finance platform.

## Proof points (use consistently)

- **90%+ forecast accuracy within two quarters** (from ~70% typical starting point → +20 points).
- **Pipeline-review prep: ~6 hours → ~30 minutes** per week.
- **~12% quota-attainment lift** within a year (with Rep Productivity).
- **4–6 week** implementation.

## Against the competition (one line each)

- vs **Clari** — warehouse-native, transparent, mid-market-priced; not a heavy enterprise black box.
- vs **Gong Forecast** — purpose-built for revenue data; you don't have to adopt a whole conversation-intelligence platform to get a real forecast.
- vs **Mosaic** — owned by the revenue team, not finance; operational, not just FP&A planning.
- vs **Pigment** — opinionated and fast-to-value for RevOps; not a blank modeling canvas you must configure for months.

Full detail in the battlecards: [Clari](battlecard-clari.md), [Gong](battlecard-gong.md), [Mosaic](battlecard-mosaic.md), [Pigment](battlecard-pigment.md).

## Words we use / avoid

- **Use:** "source of truth," "warehouse-native," "forecast accuracy," "pipeline hygiene," "transparent," "RevOps analytics layer."
- **Avoid:** "AI that replaces your forecast" (we augment, not replace), "rip and replace your BI" (we complement it), "enterprise-grade complexity."
