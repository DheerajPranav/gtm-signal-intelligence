# Discovery Questions

Use these to qualify against the [ICP](icp-definition.md) and surface the pains Northstar's [three modules](../product/overview.md) solve. Organized by theme; you won't ask all of them.

## Situation & fit (qualify the ICP)

- How many people are on the revenue team today, and how fast is it growing?
- What CRM are you on — Salesforce or HubSpot? Do you have a data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery)?
- Who owns RevOps or Sales Ops today? Is that a recent hire?
- Where does revenue reporting live right now — the CRM, a BI tool, spreadsheets, some mix?

## Forecasting pain

- Walk me through how you build the forecast each week. How long does it take?
- How accurate has your forecast been over the last few quarters? How do you even measure that?
- When the forecast is wrong, how do you find out why?
- Does leadership trust the number today? What happens on the board call when it's off?

## Pipeline hygiene pain

- How do you know if a deal is real vs stuck? Whose job is pipeline hygiene?
- How often do deals slip out of the quarter, and when do you usually find out?
- How much of your pipeline review is spent cleaning data vs actually inspecting deals?

## Rep productivity pain

- How do you know which rep activities actually create pipeline?
- How long does a new rep take to ramp, and how do you track it?
- Is quota attainment spread across the team or carried by a few reps?

## Impact & urgency

- If review prep dropped from six hours to thirty minutes a week, what would that free up?
- What's driving the timing — a new CRO, a funding round, a bad quarter, a hiring push?
- Who else feels this pain — the CRO, VP Sales, the data team?

## Decision process

- Who would need to be involved to evaluate something like this? (Map to [personas](icp-definition.md).)
- What does a security review look like on your side? (Warehouse-native usually helps — see [security](../product/security.md).)
- What's your timeline, and is there budget allocated or would we need to build the case?

## Great signals to listen for

- "We forecast in spreadsheets." "The numbers never reconcile." "We just hired a RevOps lead."
- "We're on Salesforce and Snowflake." "The board wants better forecast discipline."

These map directly to the behavioral signals in the [ICP](icp-definition.md) and set up [objection handling](objection-handling.md).
