# Objection Handling

Common objections and how to respond. Always acknowledge, reframe, and point to proof.

## "We already have Clari / a forecasting tool."

Acknowledge it's a real category. Reframe: most Clari deployments are enterprise-priced, activity-capture-heavy black boxes, and slow to change. Northstar is **warehouse-native and transparent** — every forecast number traces to the deals behind it — and deploys in **4–6 weeks**. Ask: "How much do you trust the model's number today, and how fast can you change how it's calculated?" See the [Clari battlecard](battlecard-clari.md).

## "We just use Looker / Tableau / spreadsheets."

That's exactly our ICP. Reframe: general BI tools weren't built for revenue data — no pipeline hygiene scoring, no forecast submission workflow, no forecast-vs-actual scoring, and someone rebuilds it by hand every week. Northstar is purpose-built and **complements** your BI (it models in the same warehouse). Proof: review prep drops from ~6 hours to ~30 minutes.

## "We don't have a data warehouse."

Qualify honestly — this is a [disqualifier](icp-definition.md) if there's no plan. If they're adopting Snowflake/BigQuery soon, Northstar can start on the CRM and light up warehouse-native later. If there's truly no warehouse and no plan, we're candid that we may be early for them.

## "It's not the right time / no budget."

Uncover the trigger. Often timing IS the driver — new CRO, funding round, a missed quarter. Offer the **14-day proof-of-value pilot** on a slice of live pipeline with a success metric agreed up front, so the case is built on their data, not our demo. See [pricing](../product/pricing.md).

## "How do I know the AI forecast is trustworthy?"

Reframe the AI as a **transparent second opinion**, not a replacement for the human commit. Every predicted number expands to the deals and weights behind it, and Northstar **scores every past forecast against actuals** so accuracy is measured, not asserted. See [Forecast Accuracy](../product/module-forecast-accuracy.md).

## "Security will take forever."

This is often a *strength*. On Enterprise, Northstar is **warehouse-native — your data never leaves Snowflake/BigQuery** — which shrinks the review. We provide SOC 2 Type II, a completed security questionnaire, DPA, and pen-test summary. See [security](../product/security.md).

## "You're too small / early."

Acknowledge we're focused, not everything-to-everyone. That focus is the point: we're opinionated for RevOps at your stage, not a generic enterprise or finance platform you'll spend months configuring (see the [Pigment](battlecard-pigment.md) and [Mosaic](battlecard-mosaic.md) battlecards). Point to [case studies](../case-studies/series-c-fintech.md) at similar stage and scale.
