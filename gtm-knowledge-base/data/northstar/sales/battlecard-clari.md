# Battlecard: Northstar vs Clari

## Who they are

Clari is the category-defining **revenue platform / forecasting incumbent**, strong in the enterprise. Broad suite (forecasting, pipeline, activity capture, revenue orchestration) with a large install base.

## Where they win

- Big-enterprise brand credibility and a mature enterprise feature set.
- Activity capture ("auto-captured" emails/meetings) appeals to teams that want it turned on everywhere.
- Well-known to CROs; a "nobody got fired for buying Clari" default at the high end.

## Where we win (lead with these)

- **Warehouse-native & transparent.** Northstar models in your Snowflake/BigQuery and every forecast number traces to the deals behind it. Clari's model is widely perceived as a **black box**.
- **Mid-market pricing.** Northstar Core/Growth land well under six figures; Clari is typically a six-figure enterprise commitment. For a 200–2000-employee company, Clari is often overkill and overpriced.
- **Time to value.** Northstar deploys in **4–6 weeks**; Clari implementations frequently run a quarter or more.
- **Data ownership.** On Enterprise, Northstar's warehouse-native deployment means **data never leaves your warehouse** — faster [security review](../product/security.md). Clari's activity-capture model copies more data into their platform.
- **Focused, not sprawling.** Three tight modules for RevOps at this stage vs a broad platform you grow into.

## Traps to set

- "How transparent is your forecast model — can you see exactly which deals drive the number and change the logic yourself?"
- "What did implementation actually take, start to finish?"
- "How does the activity-capture agent affect your security review and data residency?"

## Objections you'll hear

- *"Clari is the standard."* — For enterprise, sure. For a Series B–D team, it's heavy and expensive; you'd be buying a platform for a stage you're not at yet.
- *"Clari has more features."* — Breadth you may never use. Northstar is opinionated for the pipeline/forecast/productivity job you have today.

## One-liner

**Clari is the enterprise black box; Northstar is the warehouse-native, transparent, mid-market RevOps analytics layer.**
