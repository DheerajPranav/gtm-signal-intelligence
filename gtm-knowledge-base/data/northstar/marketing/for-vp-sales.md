# Northstar for the VP of Sales

## Hit the number — and know why you did.

You care about two things: a clean pipeline you can inspect and a team where more than a few reps hit quota. Northstar gives you both without turning your reps into data-entry clerks or making you wait a quarter for a rollout.

## What you get

- **Pipeline you can trust at a glance.** [Hygiene scoring](../product/module-pipeline-analytics.md) surfaces stale, stalled, and at-risk deals before the review, so review time goes to strategy, not clean-up.
- **A rep-facing forecast that isn't a tax.** Reps commit in a simple [submission workflow](../product/module-forecast-accuracy.md); the AI gives a transparent second opinion instead of overriding their judgment.
- **Coaching grounded in what works.** [Rep Productivity](../product/module-rep-productivity.md) shows which activities actually create pipeline, so you can coach the middle of the team toward what your top reps do — and ramp new reps faster.
- **More of the team at quota.** Cliniva lifted quota attainment **+12%** in a year and cut new-rep ramp by **~3 weeks**.

## Why it lands with sales leaders

It complements the tools your reps already live in — Salesforce/HubSpot, and even [Gong](../product/integrations.md) — rather than replacing them. No rip-and-replace, no six-month configuration project. See how we [position against the field](../sales/positioning.md).
