# Northstar Analytics

## The RevOps analytics layer for teams outgrowing spreadsheets.

Your CRM has the deals. Your warehouse has the data. Your forecast is held together with a spreadsheet and hope. Northstar sits on top of Salesforce/HubSpot and Snowflake/BigQuery to give revenue teams one trustworthy source of truth for **pipeline hygiene, forecasting, and rep productivity**.

[See how it works](../product/overview.md) · [Read the pricing](../product/pricing.md)

## Why teams switch to Northstar

- **A forecast you can trust.** Transparent and AI-assisted — every number traces to the deals behind it, and we score accuracy against actuals over time. Customers reach **90%+ forecast accuracy within two quarters**.
- **Reviews in minutes, not hours.** Pipeline-review prep drops from **~6 hours to ~30 minutes** a week.
- **Warehouse-native.** Runs on the data you already trust. On Enterprise, your data never leaves your warehouse — so [security review](../product/security.md) is fast and deployment takes just **4–6 weeks**.
- **Built for RevOps, priced for the mid-market.** Opinionated for Series B–D revenue teams, not a heavyweight enterprise or finance platform.

## The three modules

1. **[Pipeline Analytics](../product/module-pipeline-analytics.md)** — hygiene scoring, deal inspection, one pipeline source of truth.
2. **[Forecast Accuracy](../product/module-forecast-accuracy.md)** — submission workflow, transparent AI forecast, forecast-vs-actual scoring.
3. **[Rep Productivity](../product/module-rep-productivity.md)** — see which activities create pipeline and shorten new-rep ramp.

## Proof

Ledgerly went from **68% to 91%** forecast accuracy in two quarters. Cliniva lifted quota attainment **+12%** and cut ramp by **~3 weeks**. [Read the case studies](../case-studies/series-c-fintech.md).
