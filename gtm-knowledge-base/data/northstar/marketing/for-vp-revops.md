# Northstar for the VP of RevOps

## You were hired to make revenue predictable. Northstar is how.

You just took over RevOps (or you're building it from scratch). Leadership wants a forecast they can trust and a pipeline that's actually clean — and right now it all runs through a spreadsheet only you understand. Northstar gives you the [analytics layer](../product/overview.md) to run revenue like a system, not a fire drill.

## What you get

- **One source of truth.** Stop reconciling CRM exports and warehouse tables by hand. Northstar models both and gives you [pipeline hygiene](../product/module-pipeline-analytics.md) everyone agrees on.
- **A forecast you can defend.** Every number traces to deals and weights, and Northstar [scores every past forecast against actuals](../product/module-forecast-accuracy.md) — so accuracy is measured, not asserted.
- **Hours back every week.** Review prep drops from **~6 hours to ~30 minutes**.
- **A fast, clean rollout.** Warehouse-native means your data team says yes and [security](../product/security.md) clears quickly — live in **4–6 weeks**.

## Why RevOps leaders pick us over the alternatives

You don't need a six-figure enterprise platform (Clari) or a finance tool you don't own (Mosaic) or a blank modeling canvas you'll configure for months (Pigment). You need something opinionated for your stage that you control. See the [positioning](../sales/positioning.md).

## Proof from people in your seat

Ledgerly's VP RevOps took forecast accuracy from **68% to 91%** in two quarters. Forgestack's first RevOps hire stood up the whole function in **5 weeks**. [Read the stories](../case-studies/series-c-fintech.md).
